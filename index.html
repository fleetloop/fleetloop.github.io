FROM ANGLERFISH TO ZEBRA LIONFISH, say hello to the most diverse delights in the deep blue sea! First-time picture book author/illustrator Craig Macnaughton takes you deep under the ocean to your learn your A-B-C’s with a colourful collection of fascinating fish, impossible invertebrates and marvellous mammals. The playful illustrations and fun factoids are guaranteed to start more than a few bedtime conversations.

CRAIG MACNAUGHTON is a stay-at-home-parent, multi-disciplinary artist and film buff. “A B Sea Creatures” is his first picture book as an author/illustrator, with many more on the way! He is also a budding chef, graphic designer and award-winning filmmaker, and a member of The Forest Hill Art Club and CANSCAIP (Canadian Society of Children's Authors, Illustrators and Performers). Craig lives in Toronto with his two toddlers and understanding wife.

LITTLE MITTEN PRESS is an independent Toronto-based publisher of mischievous, creative and thoughtful children’s books. 2019 is our inaugural year, with the release of Craig Macnaughton’s first picture book, “A B Sea Creatures.” Our name comes from Craig’s three year old daughter, who, despite it being the coldest time of the Canadian winter, asks if she can wear her “little mittens.” And yes, a picture book is in the works about this struggle between a daddy and his stubborn daughter.
